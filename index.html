About

Rabblefish performs an intoxicating mix of jazz, funk, rock, blues, and R&B, featuring original compositions, heavy grooves, and virtuoso players. Guitarist/composer Chris Malley leads the band, accompanied by a rotating lineup of Colorado's finest musicians.
