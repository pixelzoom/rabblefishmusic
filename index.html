About

Rabblefish plays a vibrant mix of jazz, funk and blues, ranging from progressive to old school. The music features original compositions, with a heavy emphasis on groove. Guitarist/composer Chris Malley (formerly of Purple Squirrel) leads the band, which features a rotating lineup of Colorado's finest musicians.
